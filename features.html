Scheduling
Visual Scheduler

The state-of-the-art scheduling area in Xbiz gives you an intuitive visual representation of your classes. There are three different views, the daily schedule, weekly, and staff schedule. Each schedule can be separated by venue. You can easily colour code your classes and filter them by type. Managing your classes could not be easier.

Classes

Xbiz allows you to manage your classes easily. With a few simple clicks you can setup a class and enrol your students. You can schedule your classes any way you like – weekly, daily, or a custom setup. Your classes can be linked to terms or semesters and staff. One-off schedule changes can also be made quickly.

Make-up Classes

When a student misses a class and is marked absent, they are automatically added to a make-up class list. The student can easily be re-enrolled into a future class. You can configure a make-up policy and Xbiz will let you know how many make-up classes are owing to a student.

Event Planner

Our event planner allows you to set-up other events. You can configure the individual event’s sessions, include participants and set times and durations.

Texting

The ability to text customers & students directly to mobile phones through the software is one of ThinkSmart’s most powerful and highly used features. With the texting function, you no longer have to scan through phonebooks, spread sheets or even enrolment forms in order to contact customers. Online Components
Customer Portal

Customers have the option to manage every aspect of their account using the portal. That includes enrolments, payments, notifying of an absence, booking a make-up class, tracking their child’s progress, receiving automated messages, and more! Now you can free up your time to focus on serving customers instead of chasing them.

Online Class Registration

The aim is to reduce time spent in administration. Answering phones and adding new enrolments into your system takes up a lot of time. With our online class registration, current and potential customers are able to register and reserve spaces in a class directly through your website. You will then be notified instantly and from there staff members can automatically add all the details straight into the account and enroll the student straight into a class. The feature is an added benefit for people viewing your website and will also save the staff members a lot of time.

Here are the benefits of using Online Class Registration:

  • Saving time spent on manual data entry
  • Increase business efficiency
  • Fast and easy sales
  • Extra service for customers
Online Bookings

Our online bookings add-on allows your customers to book a studio online and pay for it immediately via a secure payment page. This can help ease the burden of over-the-counter administration. Online bookings can also dramatically improve the efficiency of your business and cut costs as staff do not need to be answering phone calls to take bookings.

A live studio schedule is published on your website which then allows your customers to book any free studio and pay online via credit card. The online bookings add-on is an extremely powerful addition to any business that wants to streamline their booking process.
